# Plant Comparative Genomics Specifics

## Major WGD Events in Crops

| Species | Recent WGD | Age (Mya) | Ks Peak |
|---------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Rice | ρ (grass) | ~70 | ~0.9 |
| Maize | ρ + recent allotetraploid | ~70 + ~12 | ~0.9 + ~0.2 |
| Wheat | ρ + two hexaploid events | ~70 + ~0.5 + ~8 | Multiple |
| Soybean | Two WGD | ~13, ~58 | ~0.1, ~0.5 |
| Cotton | ρ + allotetraploid (At/Dt) | ~70 + ~1.5 | ~0.9 + ~0.05 |
| Arabidopsis | α + β + γ | ~50, ~130, ~170 | ~0.7, ~1.3, ~1.8 |
| Brassica | α + β + γ + recent triplication | ~50, ~130, ~170, ~15 | Multiple |
| Potato | ρ + recent autotetraploid | ~70, recent | ~0.9 |

## Subgenome Dominance

In allopolyploids, one subgenome often dominates expression and gene retention:
- **Wheat**: B subgenome > A > D
- **Cotton**: At (A genome) > Dt (D genome)
- **Brassica napus**: A subgenome > C subgenome (in some tissues)
